Steve-O Engaged to Girlfriend Lux Wright: See the Gorgeous Ring!

July 2024 · 2 minute read

A Jackass star is tying the knot!

Steve-O announced on Instagram that he popped the question to girlfriend Lux Wright over the weekend, and shared a snap of the gorgeous engagement ring. Steve-O, whose real name is Stephen Glover, shared that he proposed in front of family and a few of his close Jackass pals.

"Last night, in front of a bunch of my Jackass buddies, my closest friends, my dad, and my sister... she said 'yes,'" he wrote. "I planned that for more than six months, and I’m so happy -- I can’t wait for you all to see it... #GNARLY @luxalot."

Wright also Instagrammed a pic of the two kissing to announce the good news.

"Last night was the absolute best!" she wrote. "I can’t wait to spend the rest of my life with you❤️ @steveo ❤️ #yes #duh."

This will mark Steve-O's third marriage. The 43-year-old MTV star was previously married to Candy-Jane Tucker from 2002 to 2003 and Brittany Mcgraw from 2006 to 2008. He also dated Kat Von D in 2015, though the two split after three months of dating.
