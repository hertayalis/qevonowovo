Trish Stratus Shows Off After Chasing 2021 Booty Goals

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Trish Stratus is one of the most legendary women to ever compete in WWE. Fans took to Trish from the time she debuted in the early 2000’s. Trish was welcomed back with open arms in 2018 as she made a brief return to wrestling. Stratus has made sporadic appearances since then. Most of the time, Stratus is looking to work on her fitness.

Trish worked as a fitness model before her wrestling career. Several magazines featured her as a cover model. Staying in shape has always been a part of Total Stratusfaction.

In 2021, Trish decided to work on one specific part of her body. Stratus noted that she wasn’t active enough during the pandemic lockdowns of 2020. The goal was to build her booty. The WWE Hall of Famer showed off the results in a new Instagram photo.

“One of my 2021 goals was to build my booty after doing nothing but sit on it for most of 2020 (please note and appreciate the good lighting and glute squeezing pose here)

PS fav [peach emoji] exercises are utilizing squat variations + old school donkey kicks!”

Several former WWE Superstars have been announced to return for the upcoming WWE Royal Rumble. Even Knockouts Champion and former rival of Trish Stratus, Mickie James, will compete. It’s not too late to rule her out, but so far, Trish is happy to work on her peach.
