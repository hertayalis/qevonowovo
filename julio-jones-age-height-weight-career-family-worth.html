Who Is Julio Jones?, Age, Height, Weight, Career, Family, Net Worth: Julio Jones, also known as Quintorris Lopez “Julio” Jones Jr. is an American football wide receiver.

He developed a passion for playing American football at a younger age, has been consistent throughout his career, and has become one of the most sought-after American footballers

At this time of writing (Wednesday, 18 October 2023), Jones is a football wide receiver for the Philadelphia Eagles of the National Football League

He played college football at Alabama, was regarded as one of the greatest receivers of the 2010s, and was selected by the Atlanta Falcons in the 2011 NFL Draft.

The Alabama graduate was the number six overall pick in the 2011 Draft by the Atlanta Falcons, where he played his first 10 seasons.

He has been named to the Pro Bowl seven times, was twice named to the All-Pro First Team, and led the league in yards gained in 2015 and 2018.

On Sunday, November 11, 2018, Jones became the fastest player in NFL history to reach 10,000 career receiving yards.

In 2019, Jones became the all-time career receiving yards leader for the Atlanta Falcons and in 2020 he became the all-time career receptions leader for the team.

After the 2020 season, Jones was traded to the Tennessee Titans in 2021 and after being released by the team, he joined the Tampa Bay Buccaneers in 2022.

Jones played 10 games for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers. At the end of the season, Jones did not resign and became an unrestricted free agent.

In his career, he has 903 receptions, 13,629 yards, and 81 touchdowns. In October 2023, Jones hit the headlines after he returned to the NFL.
