A former fullback/linebacker in the Arena Football League, Leroy Thompson (born January 23, 1971) played for 13 years as a fullback/linebacker. He is presently the defensive line coach for the Pittsburgh Power of the National Football League. The Albany / Indiana Firebirds (1996–2004), the New Orleans VooDoo (2005), and the Columbus Destroyers (2006–2008) were among the teams with which he played. During his collegiate football career at Delaware State University, he was named to the first team of the Division I-AA All-Americans and owns the school’s all-time quarterback sack record. In 1996, he was a member of the Philadelphia Eagles of the National Football League, and in 1994, he was a member of the Toronto Argonauts of the Canadian Football League.
