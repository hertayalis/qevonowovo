Julia Roberts debuts stunning new look: fans praise the 55-year-old actress' fresh style

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Academy Award-winning actress Julia Roberts recently unveiled a fabulous new look, and fans are absolutely loving it. 

On Tuesday her stylist Elizabeth Stewart took to Instagram to share a photo of Julia's eye-catching transformation, featuring gorgeous brunette bangs cut just below her eyebrows, seamlessly blending into her wavy locks.

In the photo, the Ticket to Paradise actress posed confidently, flashing her famous smile. 

Standing by her side, hairstylist Serge Normant put the finishing touches on her hair. 

For the makeup, artist Genevieve Herr crafted a sensuous look complete with glossy lips, smokey eyeshadow, and dramatic eyeliner.

Julia looked sensational in a form-fitting, long-sleeved black turtleneck dress by Victoria Beckham, which she accessorized with dazzling green jewelry by Chopard.

The new hairstyle was created for a Watches & Watches campaign, with Julia serving as the ambassador for Chopard's new line of recycled steel watches in Switzerland. 

Her stylist captioned the post, "@sergenormant banging out bangs in Geneva with @juliaroberts who is wearing a glorious necklace and ring by @chopard. Makeup by @genevieveherr."

Fans couldn't help but express their admiration for the Money Monster star's chic new hairdo. 

Comments flooded in, praising Julia's fresh look, with one user writing, "Bangs are back! Julia is looking so gorgeous!" Another chimed in, "STOP IT! This is so good!" while a third simply said, "She's so pretty WOW."

Julia also shared a picture on her own social media account, where she displayed her new style in a sultry photo. 

In the image, posted on Monday, the Charlie Wilson's War actress leaned against a red table, striking a pose in a stylish lavender Nells Nelson suit. 

The ensemble was perfectly accessorized with a watch and delicate bracelets. Julia captioned the post, "'Wanna Buy a Watch?' Thank you Geneva and @Chopard."

This isn't the first time hairstylist Serge has worked his magic on Julia's hair. 

One of his most memorable creations was the voluminous hairstyle he crafted for the actress at the Cannes Film Festival in May 2022. 

For the glamorous event, Julia paired her show-stopping hairstyle with a navy Louis Vuitton blazer and matching pants, once again proving her timeless sense of style.
