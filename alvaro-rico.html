Meet Sky High The Series Actor Álvaro Rico

Álvaro Rico (age: 26 years old, born on August 13, 1996) is a rising actor in the Spanish acting industry and has already gained a reputation for his impressive acting skills both on screen and on stage. Since his acting career began in 2011, he has been a force to be reckoned with in the entertainment industry.

Perhaps you recognize Álvaro from his breakout role as Nicolás in the 2017 television series “Velvet Colección.” However, his latest project is the Netflix hit “Sky High: The Series,” where he plays the character of Fernan.

With this new role, Rico is gaining even more fans and solidifying his place as one of the most talented actors of his generation.

Rico’s star is only set to rise further with his upcoming film “Invasión,” currently in post-production. With his natural talent and charm, there is no doubt that Álvaro is a name to watch in the acting world.

Who are Álvaro Rico’s Parents?

Alvaro Rico Ladera was born in Montalbán Town in Spain on August 13, 1996, to hard-working parents who owned a pastry shop. His father worked in a shoe factory, while his mother overlocked gauze for hospitals, both from the comfort of their home.

However, when the 2008 crisis hit Spain, their family business suffered, prompting them to venture into other companies, such as a nightclub, a cocktail bar, and a pastry shop, which remains their primary source of income to this day.

Despite his parents’ lack of interest in the entertainment industry, Rico grew up with a passion for the arts, thanks to his parents’ encouragement to develop values, curiosities, and socialization. He was never forced to pursue acting, but he became enamored with the idea after studying at Real Escuela Superior de Arte Dramático (RESAD) in Madrid at the age of 17.

Álvaro’s parents did not initially support his decision to become an actor, believing it was just a hobby. However, his passion for acting grew, and he pursued it relentlessly, eventually landing him roles in various Spanish television series and films.

Álvaro Rico Acting Journey

Rico began his journey on stage as a theater artist performing in La Celestina. After years of hard work and dedication, he got his big break in 2017, landing the role of Diego Bravo in “Centro médico” and Nicolás in the Velvet Collection.

However, it was in 2018 that his career skyrocketed when he landed the role of “Leopoldo “Polo” Benavent Villada” in the hit Netflix series “Elite.”

“Elite” became a game-changer for Álvaro, gaining him a massive fan following and immense recognition. Since then, Rico has continued to make his mark in the industry with his impressive performances in shows like La caza, “Monteperdido,” “Alba, El Cid,” “Madres,” “Amor y vida” and “Holy Family.”
