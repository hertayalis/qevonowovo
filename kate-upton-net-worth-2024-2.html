Early Life and Career Beginnings

Kate Upton was born in St. Joseph, Michigan, and raised in Melbourne, Florida. She was discovered at a casting call in Miami, Florida, and signed by Elite Model Management in 2008. Her career took off when she moved to New York City and signed with IMG Models.

Modeling Career

Upton’s modeling career began with work for Garage and Dooney & Bourke. She rose to prominence after appearing on the cover of the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue in 2011, 2012, and 2017. Her modeling assignments have included campaigns for Guess, Victoria’s Secret, and Bobbi Brown cosmetics.

Acting Career

Upton made her acting debut in the film “Tower Heist” in 2011. She has since appeared in films like “The Other Woman”, “The Layover”, and “Wild Man”. Her acting roles have contributed significantly to her net worth.
